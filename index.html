CHILDREN OF WAR

A thousand battles we fought.

And we forgot not to gloat !

But escaped our “infallible” sight

the violence we wrought.

We lost ourselves in our might.

To look at our side, it never occurred!

We saw not the loss we incurred!

Demons of bloodlust we inadvertently bred.

We showed to them bestial bloodshed!

Our own childrens, we gave armaments.

Knaves, we clad in opulent ornaments.

A life of vendetta we made them pursue.

With aberrant animosity, they grew…

People of their own, they love and nurture.

But “others” they know only to torture.

Incendiary taradiddles and innuendoes, they spew.

They turn hoary, this world that is tranquil and new.

In this macrocosm of formosity,

they spew seeds of calamity.

Where we see verdancy and venust

they see apostasy, decadence and lust.

Ideas that are alien, they reject and vilify.

What they see as right, they perpetually glorify.

Their innocent minds, with evils we stained.

To lives of violence, we had them chained.

We believed they’d bring heaven on earth

but taught them to bring hell to every hearth!!!

With idiocy, canards we spread.

We made them hate even the dead.

Now relish your sanguinary harvest

and over carcasses, languidly rest.

Now ponder over your folly and lament

while unspeakable atrocities they foment!

Now, where are your benevolent omnipotent gods

for whom, you made them embark radical roads?

Now where are your leaders who led you

to this state that even your enemies rue?

-Viswas Paul Johny
