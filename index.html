  • St. Antony's Public School is situated at Anakkal, two kilometers to the north east of Kanjirapally, on the Kanjirapally- Erattupetta road. It is owned and managed by St. Antony's Catholic Church, Anakkal, the vicar of the church being the manager. The Bishop of Kanjirapally is the patron of theSchool.
  • It is a co-educational institution affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and follows the text books and guidelines of the National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT). The medium of instruction is English.
  • The academic year begins in May and ends in March. Regular classes for classes VIII, IX, X and XII begin in the 1st week of May; the remaining classes (I - VII) starts in the 1st week of June. The pre-primary section opens in June.
  • The school works from Monday through Friday, the instructional time being 9.00 a.m to 3.30 p.m. There will be a minimum of 210 working days a year. The school office functions from 9.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m on all working days.
